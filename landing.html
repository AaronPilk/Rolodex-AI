Frequently asked.

Will my friends know it's an AI?
No. Messages send from your real phone number as real iMessages, written in your voice using context only you know about them. They reply to you. You read it. You respond. Rolodex never inserts itself.
How does it actually send messages?
Rolodex runs as a Mac app and uses Apple's Messages framework to send iMessages from your number — the same way Messages.app does it. You approve every message before it goes out. Messages sync to your iPhone via iCloud automatically.
Do I need a Mac?
For full iMessage send: yes. iOS doesn't allow third-party apps to send SMS or iMessage in the background — that's an Apple restriction we can't work around. Pro and Teams plans include email and LinkedIn outreach for non-Mac users.
Where does my data live?
Locally on your Mac, encrypted at rest. Cloud sync is opt-in (end-to-end encrypted). Your contact list is never shared, sold, or used to train models. SOC 2 audited.
Can I import from my CRM?
Yes. Pro supports CSV import and direct sync with HubSpot, Salesforce, Apollo, and Sales Navigator. Teams plan adds bidirectional sync.

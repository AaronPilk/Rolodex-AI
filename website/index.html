What PILK does

Most of us lose touch with the people we love not because we don't care — but because life is loud. PILK is the quiet system that keeps you connected.

💬

Reads every channel

iMessage, Instagram DMs, Facebook Messenger, Telegram, WhatsApp, email. One feed of your entire relationship surface.

🎯

Knows who's drifting

PILK scores every relationship by how long it's been, what the conversation was about, and how close you are. Surfaces the people you'd regret losing.

✍️

Writes in your voice

Every draft sounds like you — not a chatbot. Learned from how you actually text. Approve, edit, or send with one tap.

🔒

Private by design

Your messages stay on your machine. Credentials in macOS Keychain. PILK only sends what you approve.

How it works

Five minutes of setup. Two minutes a day. The rest is automatic.

1

Connect your channels

Plug in iMessage, IG, Telegram, and any others you use. PILK reads them locally on your Mac.

2

Teach PILK your circle

Tag a few people once — family, partner, close friends — and PILK auto-tiers everyone else.

3

Get a daily nudge

Every morning, PILK surfaces 3–5 people worth reaching out to today, with a draft for each.

4

Send or skip

Approve a draft, tweak it, snooze the person, or mark them handled. PILK learns your judgment over time.
